## Usage Notes

- When writing in Turkish, use the Turkish term first with English in parentheses for technical terms: "Talebin fiyat esnekliği (price elasticity of demand)"
- Use standard symbols consistently across all lectures
- For GDP, prefer $Y$ in equations but write out GSYİH in text
