### Type 2: Calculation Problems

Apply formulas and mathematical techniques.

```markdown
### Soru / Question 2 (Hesaplama / Calculation)

Talep fonksiyonu $Q_d = 100 - 2P$ ve arz fonksiyonu $Q_s = 3P - 20$ olarak verilmiştir. Denge fiyat ve miktarını bulunuz.

Given demand $Q_d = 100 - 2P$ and supply $Q_s = 3P - 20$, find equilibrium price and quantity.

<details>
<summary>Çözüm / Solution</summary>

**Adım 1 / Step 1**: Set $Q_d = Q_s$

$$
100 - 2P = 3P - 20
$$

**Adım 2 / Step 2**: Solve for $P$

$$
120 = 5P \implies P^* = 24
$$

**Adım 3 / Step 3**: Find $Q^*$

$$
Q^* = 100 - 2(24) = 52
$$

**Cevap / Answer**: $P^* = 24$, $Q^* = 52$

</details>
```

## Difficulty Progression

Include 3-5 questions per lecture with this distribution:

| Difficulty | Count | Purpose |
|------------|-------|---------|
| Temel / Basic | 1-2 | Verify core understanding |
| Orta / Intermediate | 1-2 | Apply concepts |
| İleri / Advanced | 1 | Challenge and extend |

## Solution Format

Always include:
1. Step-by-step working
2. Final answer clearly marked
3. Economic interpretation of the result
4. Common mistakes to avoid (for difficult problems)

```markdown
<details>
<summary>Çözüm / Solution</summary>
```
